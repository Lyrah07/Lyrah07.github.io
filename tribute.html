The Going Merry

A Tribute to the Going Merry:

The Going Merry, initially known as the "Going Merry" before being renamed by Usopp, was a pirate ship belonging to the Straw Hat Pirates. It was a small but sturdy ship that played a vital role in the early adventures of the crew.

Throughout its journey, the Going Merry carried the Straw Hats through various perilous situations, symbolizing their bond of friendship and the spirit of adventure. From its construction in Syrup Village to its final moments in the waters of Enies Lobby, the Going Merry will always be remembered as an integral part of the crew's history.
